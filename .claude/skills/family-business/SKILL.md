## When writing new content

**Do:**
- Mention "family business" naturally when it fits (footer, about, booking)
- Name both David and Kristina when referring to "the team"
- Use "we" for joint statements, "David" for on-water operations, "Kristina" for communications

**Don't:**
- Write "David Goldberg's jetski rental" (not his alone)
- Call it a "company" or "agency" when "family business" is more accurate
- Separate the names with "and/or" – it's always "David & Kristina"

## Tone
Friendly and direct, like David or Kristina speaking themselves. "Du" to customers, short sentences, honest enthusiasm. Never corporate-sounding.
