# Business Identity – Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail

## 2026-04-19: Brand change — CRITICAL

The business is **no longer** presented as „Jetski Lefkada Rentals run by David & Kristina Goldberg".

The public brand is now **Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail**. Goldberg as a surname is **not used** on the website, imprint, social media or in customer communication.

## Canonical forms – use exactly

| Context | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Legal entity name | `Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail` |
| Short name | `Nero Lefkada` |
| Copyright / footer | `© 2026 Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail · All rights reserved` |
| Impressum owner | `Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail` (the company itself, not a private person) |
| Website credit | `Website by AURA Web` (Daniel's agency) |
| On-dock greeting | `David` (he is the guide customers meet) |
| Booking reply signature | `– Nero Lefkada` or `– Kristina (Nero Lefkada)` — Kristina may sign internally but not surface her surname |
| Greek | `Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail · Λυγιά, Λευκάδα` |
| German | `Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail · Lygia, Lefkada` |

## The two people (internal roles – NOT public-facing)

### David — Guide / On-dock operator
- Runs safety briefings, takes guests through the ride
- Is the WhatsApp contact: +30 695 561 2777
- **May be named publicly as „David, your guide"** (no surname)
- He is NOT listed as an owner on the website

### Kristina — Marketing, website, customer communications
- Writes booking replies, runs Instagram/social, manages the website
- **Not publicly named on the site** – signs as "Nero Lefkada" or just "Kristina" without surname in personal WhatsApp replies
- Primary Claude Code admin (this project is hers)

## What NOT to write on the site / in public copy

- ❌ „David Goldberg"
- ❌ „Kristina Goldberg"
- ❌ „Family business run by David & Kristina"
- ❌ „Family-owned since 2019 by the Goldbergs"
- ❌ Any mention of Goldberg as a surname

## What IS fine to write

- ✅ „Nero Lefkada Rental&Retail"
- ✅ „Meet David, your guide at Lygia Port"
- ✅ „Our 10-minute briefing with David"
- ✅ „Founded 2019 in Lygia, Lefkada" (no names)

## How the retired „family business" tone can still work

Warmth stays. Mention:
- „A small team at Lygia Port"
- „Locally operated"
- „Year-round care for every Nero"

Just not with surnames.
