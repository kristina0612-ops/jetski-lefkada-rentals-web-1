# Review Response Templates – English

**Tone:** informal, warm, honest, no fluff. Signature always *– David & Kristina*.
**Length:** 2–4 sentences. Never longer than 3 lines on a phone screen.
**Variables:** `{name}` (if known), `{topic}` (weather/briefing/jetski/team/price)

---

## 5★ – Delighted

**Tone:** short thanks, one personal touch, invite them back.

### Variant A (short)
> Thanks {name}! So happy you had a great time. See you next summer! – David & Kristina

### Variant B (with topic reference)
> Thanks {name}! Great to hear you enjoyed {topic}. Next time you're on Lefkada, you know where to find us. – David & Kristina

### Variant C (no name)
> Thanks for the awesome review! This is exactly why we do it. See you on the water! – David & Kristina

---

## 4★ – Positive with a small but

**Tone:** thanks + address the specific point + brief signal of improvement.

### Variant A
> Thanks {name}! Good to know what wasn't 100% – we'll take {topic} on board. Next time will be smoother. – David & Kristina

### Variant B
> Hey {name}, thanks for the honest feedback. {topic} – that's something we'll work on. Glad you still enjoyed it overall! – David & Kristina

### Variant C (no name)
> Thanks for the review. We hear you on {topic} – noted and on the list. Hope to see you again. – David & Kristina

---

## 3★ – Mixed

**Tone:** honest, no excuses, concrete cause if known, invite direct contact.

### Variant A (weather)
> Hi {name}, thanks for the honest feedback. Sorry the wind didn't play along that day – Beaufort 4 makes it tricky out there. Hope to host you on calmer water next time. – David & Kristina

### Variant B (timing/logistics issue)
> Hey {name}, you're right – something didn't run right on our side. Thanks for saying it. If you want, WhatsApp David directly: +30 695 561 2777. – David & Kristina

### Variant C (general)
> Thanks {name}, this kind of feedback helps. {topic} – we have to do better. Next time we'll get it right. – David & Kristina

---

## 1–2★ – Critical

**Tone:** calm, factual, no defence. Invite 1-on-1 contact. Sign with names.

**⚠ Before approving: check with David first.**

### Variant A (invite direct conversation)
> Hi {name}, we're sorry to read this. We really want to know what went wrong. WhatsApp David directly: +30 695 561 2777 – we'll make it right. – David & Kristina

### Variant B (specific issue named)
> {name}, thanks for flagging this. {topic} – that's not how it should run. David will reach out directly (or message him: +30 695 561 2777). We'll fix it. – David & Kristina

### Variant C (unclear complaint)
> Hey {name}, sorry you weren't happy. From the review we're not 100% sure what the main issue was. Would you mind WhatsApping David? +30 695 561 2777. – David & Kristina

---

## What we never write

- ❌ „Dear Sir/Madam..." – too formal, not our voice
- ❌ „We sincerely apologise for any inconvenience..." – lifeless corporate speak
- ❌ Multiple exclamation marks – „Thanks!!!"
- ❌ Excuses, blaming the customer, discussing the waiver publicly
- ❌ Medical advice – always point to 112 (emergency)
- ❌ Promising new prices or discounts – belongs in a 1-on-1 conversation
