The small print, big

Questions we
love.

01

Do I need a boat license?

No. Under our briefing and supervision, no license needed. Must be 18+ with valid photo ID at pickup.

02

Is insurance included?

Yes - third-party liability up to €700.000 per Greek Law 4926/2022. We recommend personal accident insurance separately.

03

What about bad weather?

Above Beaufort 5 we cannot operate by law. Weather-grounded bookings are refunded 100% or rescheduled free.

04

Can I cancel?

Free cancellation 48h before. 48-24h is 50% refund. Under 24h non-refundable unless weather grounds us.

05

Where do I meet?

GPS coordinates with booking confirmation. Nidri marina, 10 min walk from the village. Free parking.

06

How far can I ride?

Greek Coast Guard rules: max 700m from shore, never inside swim buoys. Plenty of bays within that range.
