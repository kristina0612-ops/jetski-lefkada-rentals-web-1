The small print, big

Questions we love.

01

Do I need a boat license?

No. Under our on-site supervision and 10-minute briefing, no separate license required. Must be 18+ with valid ID.

02

Is insurance included?

Yes - third-party liability up to €700.000 per incident, required by Greek Law 4926/2022. Personal accident insurance we recommend separately.

03

What about bad weather?

Above Beaufort 5 we can't operate by law. Weather-grounded bookings are refunded 100% or rescheduled free.

04

Can I cancel?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before. 48-24h is 50% refund. Under 24h non-refundable unless weather grounds us.

05

Where do I meet?

Exact GPS coordinates sent with your booking confirmation. Main dock in Nidri, 10 min walk from the village center. Free parking.

06

How far can I ride?

Greek Coast Guard rules: max 700m from shore, never inside swim-zone buoys. Plenty of bays to explore within those rules.
